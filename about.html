Our Story

A Legacy of Warm Cups

15 years of crafting memories, one cup at a time.

Since 2010

From a Small Coffee Counter to North Goa's Cozy Stop

CAFE BHONSLE began as a modest coffee counter run by the Bhonsle family, built around one simple idea: serve honest coffee, fresh bites, and a place where people can pause without feeling rushed.

What started with a few cups of chai and coffee slowly became a neighborhood habit. Word spread through morning regulars, students, office breaks, and friends who came for one coffee and stayed for conversation.

Today, CAFE BHONSLE serves specialty coffee, iced drinks, sandwiches, pastries, and desserts to thousands of happy guests. But our core remains the same: fresh food, thoughtful coffee, served with warmth.

— The Bhonsle Family

Our Journey

Milestones Along the Way

2010

The Beginning

The first CAFE BHONSLE counter opens with fresh chai, coffee, sandwiches, and a handful of regular customers.

2014

First 10,000 Customers

Crossed 10K customers, expanded seating, and added iced coffee, croissants, and quick cafe bites.

2018

Cafe Favorite

Became a regular stop for coffee breaks, meetups, and takeaway orders. Launched Swiggy and Zomato delivery.

2024

50,000+ Happy Customers

50,000+ happy guests, 40+ cafe favorites, a 4.6★ Google rating, and still growing with the same warmth from Day 1.
